Configure

Rules, not vibes.

Core rules

  1. No social media apps. Browser only for replies or specific checks.
  2. No YouTube app. Search only. No Shorts, no feeds, no suggestion loops.
  3. Phone used deliberately: WhatsApp, ChatGPT, maps, banking, music, reading, language training.
  4. WhatsApp capped at 30 minutes of checking/texting. Meaningful calls do not count.
  5. Calls are unlimited. Real connection is promoted.
  6. Passive entertainment only on weekends, under two hours per day.
  7. At least 30 minutes of slow reading every day.
  8. Write a short text every day.
  9. Five minutes doing absolutely nothing.
  10. Phone away when possible. Alarm exception at night is allowed.
  11. Breaking a rule does not cancel the project. Return immediately.
